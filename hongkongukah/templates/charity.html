HongKongukah #9 is raising money for the ACLU of Massachusetts .

The need to protect the civil liberties of citizens and individuals in our country has never been greater. This Pride, we also acknowledge and aim to support the incredible work the ACLU of Massachusetts has and continues to do in upholding LGBTQ+ rights.
